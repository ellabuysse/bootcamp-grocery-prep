Avocado Toast

I was hesitent about trying avocado toast at first, but now, I am in love! To get it right, make sure to add lemon, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes for some kick. Adding an egg will make this a more hearty breakfast that will be a great start to your morning!

Ingredients

  • 1 slice sourdough bread
  • 1/2 an avocado
  • Juice from 1/4 lemon
  • 1 egg
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Fry the egg in an oiled skillet.
  2. Toast the slice of bread.
  3. Mash the avocado with the lemon juice.
  4. Top the toasted bread with the avocado mix, egg, and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes. Add basil for garnish.
