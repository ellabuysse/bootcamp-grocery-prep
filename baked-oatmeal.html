Apple Berry Baked Oatmeal

What's better than oatmeal? Baked oatmeal! I love this recipe because you can throw everything into a pan, bake in the oven, and you have a sweet breakfast that tastes like dessert! Once you try this recipe, you will want to make it again and again.

Ingredients

  • 1 c. oat milk
  • 1 T. chia seeds
  • Juice of 1/4 orange
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/4 c. chopped pecans
  • 1/4 c. coconut flakes
  • 1/2 tsp. orange zest
  • 1/2 chopped apple
  • 1 c. frozen mixed berries

Instructions

  1. Combine the wet ingredients and chia seeds in one bowl and set aside.
  2. In another bowl, mix the dry ingredients together then add the orange zest, apple, and berries.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and stir to combine.
  4. Pour into a baking pan and bake in the oven at 375 degrees F for 45 min.
  5. Top with more oat milk or maple syrup.
