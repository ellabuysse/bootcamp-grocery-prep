Banana Pancakes

Pancakes might be one of my favorite breakfasts. With this quick recipe, you can make them everyday! The addition of fresh blueberries adds a nice tartness, but feel free to add chocolate chips instead.

Ingredients

  • 2 c. oats
  • 1 c. almond milk
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 T. maple syrup or honey
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon

Instructions

  1. In a blender, grind oats until they are a flour-like consistency.
  2. Add remaining ingredients, and blend until combined, scraping the sides of the blender as needed.
  3. Let batter sit for 5-10 min. to thicken, the longer the better.
  4. Cook on a skillet over medium heat until bubbles start to form, then flip.
