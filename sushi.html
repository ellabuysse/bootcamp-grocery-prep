Veggie Sushi

I love sushi, and with this recipe, I can make it any time I want! It's fully customizable, so feel free to add whatever you have in your fridge.

Servings:

1

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori (dried seaweed)
  • Carrots
  • Red peppers
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

Instructions

  1. Spread the rice in a thin layer on a sheet of nori, leaving about an inch of space on the left and right edges.
  2. Arrange vegetables in a line along one end.
  3. Roll the nori tightly over the vegetables and continue rolling until you reach the end.
  4. Slice the sushi roll and top with avocado if you wish.
