Sweet Potato Toast

When I saw this idea, I knew I had to try it! I love sweet potatoes, so having them for breakfast makes total sense. This recipe is great because you can add whatever you want on top. It is super easy, and I know you will love it!

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato
  • Vanilla Greek yogurt
  • Honey
  • Blueberries

Instructions

  1. Slice the sweet potato into thin slices.
  2. Microwave the sweet potato slices for 3-4 min. or until soft.
  3. Toast the sweet potato.
  4. Top with yogurt, blueberries, and honey.
