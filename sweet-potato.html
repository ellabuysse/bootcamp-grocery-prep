Breakfast Sweet Potato

When I saw this idea, I knew I had to try it! I love sweet potatoes, so having them for breakfast makes total sense. This recipe is great because you can add whatever you want on top. It is super easy, and I know you will love it!

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato
  • 1 sliced banana
  • Granola
  • Almond butter
  • Frozen berries

Instructions

  1. Cook the sweet potato the night before at 400 degrees F for 45 min. Store in an airtight container in the fridge.
  2. The next morning, reheat the sweet potato in the microwave and add toppings.
