Summer Pizza

Pizza is always so fun to make at home and is great to share with family and friends. If you're looking to try something new, definitely follow this recipe for a delicious, sweet, and summery pizza.

Servings:

1

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe pizza dough
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Frozen corn
  • Zucchini
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Form the dough into a flat, circular pizza shape.
  2. Spread ricotta cheese on top.
  3. Saute the zucchini, corn, and tomatoes, then add on top of pizza.
  4. Cook the pizza in the oven according to dough instructions, then top with parmesan cheese.
